Process of consulting a doctor
First register as an Patient and their find the doctor basedon your problem and consult him. After the consultation you have to pay consultation fee for the doctors.
Process of an doctor
First register as an doctor and wait for the responce of an admin. If the admin accepts you then select your slot for consutation and and solve the problems of patients who consult you.
Process of an Phamarcy
First register as an pharmacy and then wait for the responce from the admin.
